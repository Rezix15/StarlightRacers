Game Design Document
Name: Starlight Racers
Genre: Futuristic Racing

Game Concept
Starlight racers is a fun, engaging and challenging futuristic racing game that involves racing sci-fi space-jets with unique abilities through procedural generated interactive environments. Players can shoot lasers against other players to slow them damage and weaken their shields and additionally sacrifice a portion of shield bar to increase their speed.

Audience
The game aims to be playable by everyone for casuals and old racing fans alike.

Goals
The objective of the game is to reach first place in the grand prix mode by winning all 3 matches by becoming first place.

Character
You control a professional racer college student, who decides, as a way to pay off their debts to enter into a dangerous grand prix race to win prize money. The student uses their default space-jet “Absorber” which possesses the unique defensive ability to auto repair their shield when it gets low.

Controls
The game should offer both controller and keyboard and mouse support. The user controls the character’s space-jet either using the keyboard’s directional keys to move directionally or using the controller L-stick. Unique abilities are activated using the space bar or using the (b) button on controller. The space-jets lasers are activated using the RB (right laser) or LB button (left button) when using the controller or the Q and E buttons on keyboard. 

Camera
The user views the game in the third person viewing the game in the perspective of a camera behind the space-jet. The camera moves alongside the player at all times similar to that in third person games and also the camera displays the HUD elements onto the screen.

Core Mechanics
The core mechanics of the game include:
•	Procedural Generated Tracks
•	SpaceJet Upgrades
•	Character-centric abilities
•	Interactive maps
•	SpaceJet laser cannons
•	Levels of Difficulty
Reward System
Players are rewarded with prize money and special points depending on their race positions. First place will win the most amount of money where last place will be rewarded with the least amount. Players will be able to use the money to either purchase upgrades to their SpaceJet improving their stats or equipment.

Art and Visuals
The game aims to have a futuristic and sci-fi based level design for its tracks and environments. These would include:
•	Space map swarming with asteroids to dodge and procedural planets.
•	Underwater map swarming with fish
•	Futuristic sky city avoiding aircrafts in the way.
Key Rules
Players are recommended to avoid clashing into the walls and other players, as it weakens their shield bar and temporarily lowers their speed. This can be taken advantage of by other racers to shoot the vulnerable players down causing even more damage.
